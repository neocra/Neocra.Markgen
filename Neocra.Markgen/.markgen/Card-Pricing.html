Card Pricing

Get price card

This reference describes how to retrieve the RFID Card price thanks to its related offerId.

Path Parameter

offerId : The pricing offer Identifier in Be:Mo's system.

Important output

price : The card price (excluding tax)

vat : amount of VAT (value added tax) and other taxes that will have to be paid.
